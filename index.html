Passionate about perfection

We’re the UK’s only manufacturer of conference microphone systems. Our specialist software and equipment is based on many decades working with the United Kingdom's principal local and regional authorities. Delegate microphones, electronic voting, and webcasting are but a few of our products. As we manufacture our products in-house, we offer bespoke microphone units, in both flush mount and portable options – all available in any size, shape or colour. Plus, you can personalise it with your corporate logo too. We also offer a rental service for customers who do not require a permanent installation.
